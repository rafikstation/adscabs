Over ons

We know the road.
We built the network.

AdsCabs was not built in a boardroom. It was built by people who spent years inside the taxi and transport industry — and saw an opportunity that everyone else was driving past.

Het verhaal

From the industry, for the industry

The founders of AdsCabs come from a background in aviation, transport logistics, and mobility operations. We have managed fleets, coordinated passenger flows, and solved problems that only make sense when you've stood on the ground watching them happen.

That experience taught us one thing clearly: the taxi is one of the most underused media surfaces in the city. Thousands of vehicles. Millions of rides. A captive audience with nowhere to go and nothing to do but pay attention.

So we built AdsCabs — a network designed from the inside out, by people who understand both the vehicle and the passenger sitting in it.

De missie

Advertising that actually moves people

Most advertising competes for attention. AdsCabs earns it. Our formats — in-cabin tablet screens and full exterior taxi wraps — put brands exactly where audiences already are, without interrupting anything.

We believe advertising works best when it fits naturally into the moment. A passenger on their way to Schiphol. A tourist exploring Amsterdam for the first time. A professional heading to their next meeting. These are real people, in real places, ready to receive a real message.

"We didn't invent taxi advertising. We reinvented what it could be — measurable, targeted, and built for the brands that want more than just visibility."
Groei & netwerk

Amsterdam first. The Netherlands next.

We started where the demand is highest — Amsterdam. The city with the busiest taxi corridors, the highest tourist density, and the most premium passenger profile in the Netherlands. Every route, every driver, every screen in our network was chosen with one goal: maximum impact for every advertiser.

Begin

Network launch in Amsterdam

First taxis on the road, first screens activated. Focus on the Schiphol corridor and city center — the highest-traffic routes in the Netherlands.

Nu

Growing fleet, live campaigns

Active tablet screens and wrapped taxis running brand campaigns across Amsterdam. Real reporting. Real reach. Real results for early partners.

Binnenkort

Expansion to Rotterdam, Utrecht & beyond

The Randstad is the next step. A nationwide network is the destination. Brands that join now grow with us — and get the best rates in the process.
